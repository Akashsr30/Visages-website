    General Guidelines & Rules

    ⚠️ Important Notice for SJU Students

    St Joseph's University students are not eligible to participate in any events except Futsal, Flashmob, Battle Of Bands & Western Street Battle. This includes both individual and contingent registrations.

    General Rules

    • The contingent fee is Rs. 16,500/- (Includes one participant/team for all events, excluding open events).
    • A college must participate as a contingent to be eligible for overalls.
    • Last date for registration: 21st February 2026.
    • On-spot registration: 8.30 am to 9.30 am.
    • Valid college ID card is mandatory.
    • Participants registering under non-contingent must pay the fee for their respective event.
    • Participants are required to submit any audios a day prior via Google Forms.
    • Backup of audio tracks is mandatory for performing participants.
    • Timings will be informed by event heads in WhatsApp groups.
    • Report 30 mins before event start.
    • Judges' decision is final and binding.
    • No refund once registration is done.

    Prohibited Items

    • Illicit substances (alcohol, cigarettes, vapes, drugs).
    • Edibles and beverages (including water).
    • Sharp objects or weapons.
    • Flammable objects (perfumes, sanitizers).
    • Cosmetics (only allowed for dance/fashion).
    • Medicines without prescription.

    Dress Code

    • Girls: No sleeveless, deep necks, sheer, off-shoulder, cropped tops, or ripped jeans. Nothing above the knee.
    • Boys: No sleeveless/tank tops. No shorts, 3/4th pants, or ripped jeans.

    Code of Conduct

    • Zero-tolerance for vulgarity, misbehavior, or mention of sensitive topics (politics, religion, etc.).
    • Zero-tolerance policy towards illicit substances.
